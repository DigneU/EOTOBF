ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

We are a Fellowship of Bible believers who gathered together to teach each other the Bible in July 2020. We were inspired by the community of Jewish believers in Berea, who not only believed the word that Paul preached to them, but also looked through the Scriptures to check if what he said was true. We believe in Jesus, our Savior, the inerrancy of the Holy Bible and the leading of the Holy Spirit in all righteousness. We welcome all who want to learn the lessons of God's Word as expressed in his Word.

OUR AIM

We aim to get each member of the fellowship to be familiar with the Bible and with the themes and ideas that are expressed by the Bible. Thereby equipping everyone to be able to rightfully divide the word of Truth in Spirit & in Truth. Our desire is to spread the love of Christ by doing for the least of these, teaching all who would listen about the Gospel of Peace that saves from the Day of Judgement & encouraging each other to remain committed to Faith in Jesus Christ.
